About Us

The Healthstasia was created by a group of social workers from various backgrounds who were motivated by the concept, principle, and people of achieving peace, equality, social justice, uniformity, and self-sufficiency in Trust. It is said that Trust plays the most crucial role in Lok Sewa growth (Civil Service). As a result, it is committed to the notion of passing ownership of all its efforts to the people who will profit in the end. Because the team was concerned about the existing state of Trust in Delhi, where all places were populated and socioeconomic backwardness was noted in all critical sectors. The Healthstasia are dedicated to bringing about social change for the majority Healthstasia have blazed the path for the region's impoverished, particularly women, children, and landless farmers. As a consequence, their standard of living will improve. Healthstasia is a plan to enhance our Trust and produce wealth by providing the best health facilities and increasing understanding about health issues. To assist the impoverished in receiving high-quality primary health care.
